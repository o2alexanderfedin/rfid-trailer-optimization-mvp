## Phase Goal

**As a** logistics operator, **I want to** query where a package was, what is on a trailer, and a hub's inventory from a deterministically-replayable twin, **so that** every answer is auditable and reproducible.

<objective>
Implement `packages/projections`: the inline operational twin (package-location, trailer-state, hub-inventory) built from PURE reducers, with idempotent per-projection last-seq folds and a truncate+replay rebuild driver — gated by the keystone golden-replay test (FND-04) and the purity guard (P3). These projections answer FND-05/06/07. Idempotency is P5a; deterministic replay is P3 — both HIGH-cost to retrofit, so they are baked in now.

Purpose: This is the operational twin that every later phase reads from and the single most important correctness invariant of the foundation: live state must equal rebuilt-from-log state, byte-for-byte. Pure reducers make this testable and reusable by aggregation/optimizer later.
Output: `@mm/projections` exporting pure reducers, `applyInline`, `rebuildProjections`, and the operational projection schema.
</objective>
