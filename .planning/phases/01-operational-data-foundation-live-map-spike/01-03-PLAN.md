## Phase Goal

**As a** logistics operator, **I want to** an append-only event store with optimistic concurrency and total ordering, **so that** concurrent writers cannot corrupt history and projections can replay deterministically.

<objective>
Implement `packages/event-store`: the append-only Postgres event store with per-stream optimistic concurrency (`UNIQUE(stream_id, version)` + expected-version check, `23505` -> `ConcurrencyError` + retry), a monotonic `global_seq` total order, and `readStream`/`readAll` read paths. This is FND-01 (append-only JSONB events) and FND-02 (optimistic concurrency + total ordering), and it bakes in P4 (the sim and, later, the optimizer are concurrent writers).

Purpose: The event store is the operational twin's backbone and the source of all auditability and deterministic replay. Getting OCC + global ordering right here is HIGH-cost to retrofit. This contract (`appendToStream`/`readStream`/`readAll`) is consumed by projections (Plan 04), simulation (Plan 05), and the API (Plan 06).
Output: `@mm/event-store` exporting `appendToStream`, `readStream`, `readAll`, `ConcurrencyError`, and a `migrate` helper applying `schema.sql`.
</objective>
