<objective>
Generalize the route builder and the engine freight flow from the hard-wired single Memphis center to a
`centerOf(spoke)` model (NET-01), route freight spoke -> nearest center -> backbone -> dest center ->
dest spoke under the `continentalTopology` flag, and add a per-center scope partition to
`detectAffectedScope` (NET-05) — all while keeping the legacy 10-hub path byte-identical.

Purpose: This is the engine change the whole milestone assumes. The single hardest constraint is the
flags-off equivalence: the generalized `buildRoutes` MUST emit the IDENTICAL `Route[]` for the legacy
single-center input, and with the flag absent the seed-42 stream must not drift (the golden gate in
plan 23-05 is the witness). The per-center partition is the real scaling fix — it caps each epoch's
twin to one center's trailers.

Output: multi-center `routes.ts`, flag-gated `centerOf` engine flow, per-center `detectAffectedScope`.
</objective>
