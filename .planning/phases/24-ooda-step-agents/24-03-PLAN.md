<objective>
Make agent decisions own their BINDING LOCAL FEASIBILITY (OODA-03) — fuel, HOS/rest, and dock capacity — by REUSING the existing shared HOS engine (`@mm/domain` forward-labeling: `mayDriveNow`, `remainingLegalDriveMinutes`, `applyDrivingLeg`), the fuel odometer-threshold logic, and the consolidation/dock-capacity logic. This is explicitly "REUSE, do NOT rebuild" (CONTEXT decision OODA-03). The feasibility gate is applied INSIDE the truck/hub Decide so an infeasible outcome can NEVER leave the agent — establishing the contract a P25 coordinator cannot override (no coordinator exists yet; this phase only establishes the contract Phase 25's accept/reject arbitrates against).

Purpose: OODA-03. Without this, an agent could "decide" to proceed when out of legal hours or over the fuel threshold, producing an illegal stream and breaking the eventual coordinator's reject-with-reason ("won't divert: HOS/fuel"). The feasibility predicates are pure, deterministic, and a thin adapter over the already-tested domain engines — owning a NEW copy of HOS/fuel math would be both wasteful and a determinism-drift risk (two implementations diverging at the 1-ULP boundary).

Output: `ooda/feasibility.ts` (pure predicates reusing the domain engines), the Decide functions gated through them, and TDD tests proving (a) reuse not reimplementation, (b) infeasible outcomes are unreachable.
</objective>
